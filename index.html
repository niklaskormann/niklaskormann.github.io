About Me

I am a PhD student in the Data Science and Mining Team at the Laboratoire d'Informatique of École Polytechnique in France. Previously, I finished my master degree at Fachhochschule Wedel. I wrote my master thesis in cooperation with École Polytechnique under the supervision of Prof. Johannes F. Lutzeyer and Prof. Hendrik Annuth after a research internship at École Polytechnique. Prior to that, I finished my bachelor in Industrial Engineering at Fachhochschule Wedel.

I am interested in translational research in Deep Learning with a focus on Graph Neural Networks and tackle challenges of heterophilic graphs and generalisation. In my master thesis I studied the application of Graph Neural Networks in Histopathology.
